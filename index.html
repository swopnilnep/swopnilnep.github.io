About me

Originally from a bustling city amidst a hilly valley, I am passionate about mountain biking, hiking and travel. I believe that going to different places where you are able to experience something new pushes you beyond your comfort zones and makes you a well rounded person. I get easily intrigued when I find something new, and read quite about within different subjects and genres seeking new information and experiences. I believe that the most important skill someone can have is knowing how to learn. For anyone who wants to learn better, I really recommend this course.

I also care a lot about data. My journey with data began as I discovered the power of R and started exploring Kaggle datasets out of sheer curiosity. What I had learned was that every set of data contains a story, which is only clear after layers of cleaning, preparation and analysis. I spent my time learning the process of converting data into information through internships and personal projects. By using this data to design and train machine learning models, I realized that data can be a valuable resource for the world that will transform our lives in the coming years.

My personal goal is to use my education and experience to be a driver of this transformation. I currently work as an Engineer Apprentice at CloudFactory, Nepal.
